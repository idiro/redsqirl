Red Sqirl Pricing

Free for evaluation and non-production environments

Red Sqirl is free to download for evaluation purposes. You can use it in a non-production environment for as long as you want. However, when you move it to a production environment then a licence fee becomes payable. Licensing is very straightforward and is detailed below.

Production environment licence costs

The licence fee for Red Sqirl includes five days of support and is as follows:

Cluster size of 0-100 nodes: €50,000

Each additional 100 nodes thereafter (or fraction of): Additional €10,000
Example:

    (a) A cluster of 35 nodes costs €50,000
    (b) A cluster of 90 nodes costs €50,000
    (c) A cluster of 110 nodes costs €60,000
    (d) A cluster of 190 nodes costs €60,000
    (e) A cluster of 850 nodes costs €130,000

Licences include free point upgrades.

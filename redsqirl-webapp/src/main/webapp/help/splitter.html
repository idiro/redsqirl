Splitter

Splitter Vertical - when pressing the right mouse button a menu appears with the three options for Right, Middle, and Left. Clicking on the splitter option changes the position to Left, Middle or Right.

  • Right - The splitter move to the right
  • Middle - The splitter move to the middle
  • Left - The splitter move to the left

Splitter Horizontal - when pressing the right mouse button a menu appears with the three options for Top, Middle, and Bottom. Clicking on the splitter option changes the position to Top, Middle or Bottom.

  • Top - The splitter move to the top
  • Middle - The splitter move to the middle
  • Bottom - The splitter move to the bottom
